GAME INSTRUCTIONS

This game contains only words with no repeated letters.

Computer generates a secret 5-letter-word. Your task is to find it in 5 tries.

Enter letters on the keyboard bellow and use DEL to make corrections. Enter your word and tap ENT to submit it for evaluation.

X
a correct letter in a correct position
Y
a correct letter in an incorrect position
Z
this letter is not in a secret word

Tap anywhere at the top of the screen to close this window and start playing.

Good luck !
